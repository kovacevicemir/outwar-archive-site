    Cobalt Set

    To start the quest, talk to Waiter Assasin, in Dominiques Restaurant.

    The items you need drop from the following crew adventures:

    • Spiked Turtle - Cracked piece of Cobalt
    • Centuar Archer - Chipped piece of Cobalt
    • The Green Dragon - Flawed piece of Cobalt
    • Decayed Zombie - Smooth piece of Cobalt
    • El Diablo - Perfect piece of Cobalt

    Give all of these to Waiter Assasin, to receive 1 piece of the Cobalt set. Repeat until you have the full set

    To complete the set, you can swap items you have multiples of , for ones you need. This can be done by going to blacksmith and chosing Morph.

    Bonus: 10 atk / 50 def
