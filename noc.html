    NOC Set

    To start the quest, talk to Walshy the Bar Owner, in the Drunken Clam.

    The items you need drop from the following crew adventures:

    • Unravelling Mummy - Mummy Wrappings
    • Aboroth - Sorcerer's Staff
    • Gorefiend - Spectre Bones
    • Afflicted Soul - Trapped Soul
    • Drakin Fang - Vampire Fangs

    Give all of these to Walshy, the Bar Owner, to receive 1 piece of the NOC set. Repeat until you have the full set

    To complete the set, you can swap items you have multiples of , for ones you need. This can be done by going to blacksmith and chosing Morph.

    Bonus: 42 atk / 300 def
