Kix Woods

Talk to Lunasa
  • get 100 Mushroom dropped by Poisonous Mushroom - reward 350k xp
  • talk to Raphiqe in Pineblood village
  • talk to Pineblood Bed - Strength Potion and Rage Potion
  • talk to Raphiqe
  • get 50 Spiders Silk from Kix Spiders - 175k xp
  • go talk to Patchno at the woods entrance
  • kill 20 Maw Demon
  • talk to Patchno, go talk to Raphiqe and finaly the Campfire which unlock Gandala
  • talk to Gandala
  • talk to Pineblood Bed - Rage Potion
  • get 20 Kiro of Titonoclasim - 175k xp
  • talk to Raphiqe
  • talk to Tycloth in Bokroroo
  • get 30 Shaker Thorns from Spine Shakers - 250k xp
  • talk to Tycloth again which will send you to Gandala, then go talk to Raphiqe and back to Gandala
  • get 50 Spear (Wog Riders)and 20 Twine (hard brush) - 250k xp
  • talk to Titonoclasim Torch
  • when talking again to Gandala you'll receive Twine, Spear and Kiro of Titonoclasim to boil
  • go talk to Raphiqe and receive 350k xp + Sovereign Necklace
  • Go talk to Tycloth
  • kill 15 Diseased Bokroroo
  • Get 20 Healing Fern from Cascading Fern
  • talk to Tycloth, he'll give you a bandage. then go talk to Wounded Bokroroo a few spots lower, then the Dieing Bokroroo, the Injured Bokroroo, Hurt Bokroroo and lastly Bleeding Bokroroo. You're done, go talk to Tycloth - 250k xp
  • Go talk to Bokroroo Bed - Minor Experience Boost + Rage Potion
  • go back to Tycloth and talk to the Handwritten Note that replaces him
  • go talk to Hansack (still in Bokroroo)
  • Get 50 Kiro of Titonoclasim, 30 Spear and 25 Twine - 250k xp
  • kill 65 Diseased Bokroroo, Sickly Bokroroo and Mutated Bokroroo. 1 time spawn, they spawn everywere in Kix, if when you're done going trough you're still missing some kills it means you missed one/several spots - reward 200k xp
  • Head to the Pinebloods (Raphiqe), and then to the Eldorian (Hilliam). Go back to Hansack
  • Get 100 Mushroom and 50 Healing Fern - 200k xp
  • go talk to Hilliam and back to Hansack
  • talk to Titonoclasim Torch
  • talk to Hansack which will send you to the Bokroroo Bed - Rage Potion
  • go back to Hansack and receive a Sovereign Chestplate and send you to Hilliam
  • Get 75 Lumber (from Kix Wood Tree)
  • Receive a Battering Ram to go find tools
  • talk to Barricade
  • talk to Toolbox
  • 1 Hammer (Scattered Leaves, location in front of the toolbox), 1 Handsaw (Branch Pile, location in front of the toolbox), 1 Red Wrench (loose brush, all north location) and 1 Shovel(various items, all north location) - 200k xp
  • go back to Hilliam
  • kill 100 Diseased Kraw, 100 Mutated Kraw and 100 Sickly Kraw - 200k xp
  • Talk to Ugeth (Kraw vilage) and go back to Hilliam
  • get 50 Fleshbag meat (from  Fleshbag demons, all north location) - 200k xp
  • go talk to Bridge Worker and go back to Hilliam
  • get 50 Kiro of Titonoclasim, 30 Spear and 25 Twine - 250k xp
  • Talk to Eldorian bed which get Ugeth to popup, talk to him.
  • Kill 20 mutated Eldorians
  • Go find Hilliam which is now in the room located in front of the toolbox
  • Kill Fleshbag Giant - 200k xp
  • Go back and talk to Ugeth, talk to Hilliam which is back in Eldorian, talk to Titanoclasim Torch and finally Hilliam again. Reward: 350k xp and Sovereign Plasma Shield
  • go talk to Bridge Worker, cross the bridge and talk to Naya
  • Kill 150 Mutant hags, and 150 Death Walkers - 200k xp
  • go talk to Rindo Tan (EoB Sundernian) and back to Naya
  • Kill 150 Diseased Jaxalo, Sickly Aequora, Mutated Jaxalo and Mutated Aequora - 250k xp
  • Get 50 aequora blood samples (poisoned aequaras, aequora village) and 50 jaxalo blood samples (from poisoned jaxalos, jaxalo village) -250k xp
  • Go talk to Amelie, located in hidden path. Past the bridge, just before you enter Kraw woods there is a hidden path. Check the arrows on the map and not the map itself!
  • Get 40 twine, 40 healing ferns, 20 crushed emeralds(from Stacked rocks), 150 lumber and 200 mushrooms. - 250k xp
  • Go talk to Large Chunk of Debris (last room in this location), talk to Rindo tan, Rindo's Guest Bed - a recharge the fury
  • Go talk to Rindo Tan - Reward: 200k xp and Sovereign Helm
  • Go talk to Naya, then Amelie
  • Get 30 crushed diamonds (from Stacked rocks) -200k xp
  • Talk to the Lock of Hair that now replace Naya, go talk to Naya which is now in Jaxalo Village - 200k xp
  • Kill the Radiated Abomination that just spawned, talk to Naya again, go back and talk to the lock of hair, talk to Naya. - 200k xp
  • Kill 200 Diseased Aequora, Diseased Jaxalo, Mutated Aequora, Mutated Jaxalo, Sickly Aequora and Sickly Jaxalo - 200k xp
  • Get Clay (from Loose Rock Pile, Kraw Woods), Copper Wire (from Loose Dirt Pile, Pineblood Woods close to Titonoclasim), Power Coil (from Loose Dirt entrance to kix woods), Steel Caps (from loose rock aequora woods), Fuse(from Loose Leaves, Bokroroo Woods, at Draken). Talk to Naya then to Rindo Tan - 300k xp
  • Go talk to Ugeth (which is now in Eldorian), talk to Kraw Treasure Chest (Kraw Village). - reward: Sovereign Plasma Rifle
  • Finally go talk to Rindo Tan to finish it all off - Reward: 500k xp and a Radiation Meter.


At the entrance of the City of Eternal Star you can now read this "In the distance, you can see the gate to the City of Eternal Star. Congratulations on finishing the Kix Woods! The City of Eternal Star content pack will be released sometime in the near future! Check the Outwar news and forums for more information. Great job! Enjoy the Sovereign Set."
