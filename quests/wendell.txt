Quest: Detective Files


Start: Talk to WENDELL CUNNINGHAM, in CITY HALL
She will give you DETECTIVE FILES, and ask you to bring them to the DC DETECTIVE

Solution : Talk to the DC DETECTIVE, in DC ENFORCERS, and give him the files.
He will give you SIGNED DETECTIVE FILES.
Go back and talk to WENDELL CUNNINGHAM, and give her the signed files.


Reward: 220
