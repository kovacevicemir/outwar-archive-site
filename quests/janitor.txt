Quest: Janitor Duty

Mutli Level

Start: Talk to DUSTY, he is in the STORAGE FACILITY.
He will ask you to bring him, his JANITOR KEYS

Solution: Kill GIGANTIC DUST MITE, and COCKROACH in the STORAGE FACILITY until you receive JANITOR KEYS
go back and talk to DUSTY

He will then ask you to bring back more keys (twice more) kill GIGANTIC DUST MITE, and COCKROACH in the STORAGE FACILITY
for all keys for this quest

Reward: 11000 experience total and a Brutality potion
