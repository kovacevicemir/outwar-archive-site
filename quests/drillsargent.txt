Quest: Drill Sargent

Start: Talk to DRILL SARGENT in the TRAINING GROUNDS. He will ask you to kill:
10 WOLVES
3 ARMADILLOS

Solution: Kill  10 WOLVES and 3 ARMADILLOS in the TRAINING GROUNDS. 
Then go and talk to a DRILL SARGENT

Reward: 24 experience
