Quest: Poised Gargoyle

Start: Talk to POISED GARGOYLE,  in the SYNGE DESTRUCTION AREA.
He will ask you to kill 27 STONE BASILIKS, and collect 1 GARGOYLE WING

Solution: Kill 27 STONE BASILIKS in the SYNGE DESTRUCTION AREA and CHUGGERS PALACE BAR.
STONE BASILIKS also drop the GARGOYLE WING
go back and talk to POISED GARGOYLE

Reward: 3000 experience
