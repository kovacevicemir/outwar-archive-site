Quest: Gambling Musician


Start: Talk to ADDICTED COMPOSER in the SOUNDWEAVER ACADEMY
He will ask you for 10 CASINO CHIPS

Solution : Kill HIGH ROLLERS  in the CASINO until you have 10 CASINO CHIPS
then talk to ADDICTED COMPOSER, and give them to him


Reward: 500 experience
