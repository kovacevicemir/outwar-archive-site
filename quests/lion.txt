Quest: Coliseum Trader (Lion)


Start: Talk to COLISEUM TRADER (Lion), in the COLISEUM
He will ask for
1 UNCUT EMERALD
1 SURGEON SMOCK

Solution : For the UNCUT EMERALD, Kill ENTHUSIAST in the COLISEUM, until they drop it
For the SURGEON SMOCK, kill SURGERY TEAM in the HOSPITAL until they drop it

Go back and talk to COLISEUM TRADER (LION), and give him the items, 

Reward: LION AUG
