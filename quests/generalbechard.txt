Quest: General Bechard

Start: Spawn GENERAL BECHARD, in the TRAINING GROUNDS (do this by attacking the other 
mobs in the same room)
Once spawned talk to GENERAL BECHARD, he will ask you to kill 50 SAND TROLLS

Solution: Kill  50 SAND TROLLS . These are found in the WASTELANDS 
Once you have killed 50, respawn GENERAL BECHARD and talk to him.

Reward:  Blade of Troll Slaying 
ATK: 25
Rage/Turn: 5
Exp/Turn: 5
