Quest: Magic Beercan

Start: Talk to BARON WARSAW,  in CONSTRUCTION SITE.
He will ask you to collect the TOP PIECE OF MAP, and BOTTOM PIECE OF MAP

Solution: Kill HIGH ROLLERS in the CASINO for the TOP PIECE OF MAP
Kill MIND COMPOSERS in the SOUNDWEAVER ACADEMY for the BOTTOM PIECE OF MAP
go back and talk to BARON WARSAW

Reward: Magic Beer Can
