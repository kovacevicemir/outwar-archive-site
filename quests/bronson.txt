Quest: Bank Notes


Start: Talk to COUNCIL MEMBER BRONSON, in CITY HALL
He will ask you for one of each of these:
(security) Bank Note
(undercover) Bank Note
(vault) Bank Note

Solution : Kill UNDERCOVER THIEF, VAULT GUARD, and SECURITY GUARD, until they drop the notes
These mobs are found in BLACKHEARTS BANK

Reward:300
