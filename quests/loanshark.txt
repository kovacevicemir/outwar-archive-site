Quest: Loan Collection


Start: Talk to LOANSHARK, in BLACKHEARTS BANK
He will ask or 2 STACK OF CASH

Solution : Kill FURIOUS GAMBLERS in CASINO, until they drop 2 STACK OF CASH
Go back and talk to LOANSHARK, and give him the 2 STACK OF CASH, he will then give you another STACK OF CASH
Speak to VAULT GUARD in BLACKHEARTS BANK, and give him the cash

Reward: 1000 experience
