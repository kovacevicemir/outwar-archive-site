Quest: Blackhearts money


Start: Talk to BLACKHEART"S ASSISTANT, in BLACKHEARTS BANK
He will ask or 1 STACK OF CASH

Solution : Kill FURIOUS GAMBLERS in CASINO, until they drop 1 STACK OF CASH
Go back and talk to BLACKHEART"S ASSISTANT, and give him the 1 STACK OF CASH, 

Reward: 400 experience
