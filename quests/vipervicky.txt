Quest: VIPER VICKY

Start: Talk to VIPER VICKY BARTENDER, she is in CHUGGERS PALACE BAR.
She will ask you to kill 30 JACKHAMMER BRUTES

Solution: Kill 30 JACKHAMMER BRUTES in the CONSTRUCTION SITE
go back and talk to VIPER VICKY BARTENDER

Reward: 2000 experience
