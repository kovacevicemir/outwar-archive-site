Quest: Allarcas

Start: Talk to ALLARCAS,  in the CHUGGERS PALACE BAR.
He will ask you to collect 40 DRAGON SCALES

Solution: Kill BLAZE WYVERNS, DRAGON WELPS and FLAREWHELPS in the SYNGE DESTRUCTION AREA 
until you have 40 DRAGON SCALES
go back and talk to ALLARCAS

Reward: 4000 experience
