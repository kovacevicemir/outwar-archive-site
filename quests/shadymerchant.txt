Quest: Shady Merchant

Start: Talk to the SHADY MERCHANT, he is in the DUSTY GLASS TAVERN
He will give you the ARMS SHIPMENT, and ask you to deliver it to GANGLORD RAVELGES:

Solution: Talk to and give the ARMS SHIPMENT to GANGLORD RAVELGES, he is in RANCID'S WASTELAND CAMP

Reward: 45 experience
