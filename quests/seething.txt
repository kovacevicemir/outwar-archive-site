Quest: Seething Ruenic


Start: Talk to SEETHING RUENIC in RANCID'S WASTELAND CAMP
He will ask for:
1 MERCENARY GLASSES
1 WATER BAG

Solution : For the MERCENARY GLASSES, kill MERCANARY in RANCID'S WASTELAND CAMP
For the WATER BAG, kill DESERT RAIDER in RANCID'S WASTELAND CAMP
When you have both items, talk to  SEETHING RUENIC and give them to him

Reward: 950 experience
