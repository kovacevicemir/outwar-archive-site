Quest: Casino Manager

Start: Talk to the CASINO MANAGER, he is in the CASINO.
He will ask you to bring him, FAT TONY'S HEAD

Solution: Spawn and kill FAT TONY in the NIGHTCLUB until you receive FAT TONY'S HEAD
go back and talk to CASINO MANAGER

Reward: A Bomb
