Quest: DJ PULLOUT

Start: Talk to DJ PULLOUT,  in the NIGHTCLUB.
He will ask you to collect 4 SICK BEATS, and 1 TUBA

Solution: Kill MUSIC MENTOR and MUSIC WEAVER in the SOUNDWEAVER ACADEMY for both of the items
go back and talk to DJ PULLOUT

Reward: 500 experience
