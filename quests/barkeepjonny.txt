Quest: A Tasty Brew

Start: Talk to the bar Keep Jonny, he is in the DUSTY GLASS TAVERN
He will ask you for 6 CORPION VENOM

Solution: Kill SCOPRIONS in the TRAINING GROUNDS these will drop SCORPION VENOM
when you have 6, go back and talk to BAR KEEP JONNY

Reward: 45 experience
