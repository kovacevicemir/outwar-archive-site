Quest: Scorched Mage

Multiple levels


Start: Talk to SCORCHED MAGE, in CITY HALL
He will ask you to kill 6 TONED REBELS

Solution : Kill 6 TONED REBELS in the CONCERT HALL

Go back and talk to SCORCHED MAGE, he will now ask you to kill him
Solution: Kill SCORCHED MAGE.

Reward: 7000
