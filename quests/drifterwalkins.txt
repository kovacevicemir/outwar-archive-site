Quest: Drifter Walkins

Start: Talk to DRIFTER WALKINS, in the WASTELANDS.
He will ask you to kill:
5 BASALIKS
5 SKELETONS
5 DERVISHES

Solution: Kill  these mobs, they are also in the WASTELANDS. Once you have killed them the required
amount, talk to DRIFTER WALKINS

Reward:  35 experience
